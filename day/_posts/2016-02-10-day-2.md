# Day Recap

## The Debugging Process

Today I introduced the "innovator's compass" (created by [Ela Ben-Ur](http://i2iexperience.com/people/)) as a potential tool for framing the process of building and debugging a circuit on a breadboard. At its most basic, the compass serves as a reminder that factors "outside the breadboard" may be affecting the behavior of your circuit.
